We primarily serve Birmingham and the wider Midlands area, but we also take on projects throughout the UK. For larger projects, we're open to working internationally. Our team has experience delivering projects across Europe and the Middle East. Please contact us to discuss your specific location and project requirements.

The timeline varies depending on the project size and complexity. Here's a general guideline:

  • Large commercial/industrial projects: 3-6 months
  • Master planning projects: 6+ months

During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

To provide an accurate quote, we typically need:

  • Project location and type (residential, commercial, etc.)
  • Scope of services required (concept design, full design, etc.)
  • Any specific requirements or constraints
  • CAD drawings, CAD models, drawing packs, and all available relevant information

Initial sketches or existing drawings are helpful but not essential. We can discuss your project in detail during our initial consultation.
